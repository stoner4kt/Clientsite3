Our Simple Process

Applying for vehicle finance has never been easier. We handle the paperwork while you focus on choosing your next vehicle.

  1. Submit your application online
  2. We assess your eligibility
  3. Approval within 24 – 48 hours
  4. Drive away in your vehicle

Minimum Requirements

To qualify for vehicle finance, applicants must meet the following basic requirements:

  • ✔ Minimum salary of R12,000 per month
  • ✔ Valid South African ID
  • ✔ Valid driver’s license
  • ✔ Latest payslip
  • ✔ 3 months bank statements
