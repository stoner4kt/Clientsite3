Privacy Policy & Company Procedures

1. Information Collection

Auto Finance Group collects personal information provided voluntarily by clients through our website, application forms, and communication channels. This may include names, contact details, income information, and credit status.

2. Use of Information

Collected information is used strictly for vehicle finance assessments, client communication, and service improvement. Information is processed in compliance with applicable data protection laws.

3. Data Protection

We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect client data against unauthorized access, loss, or misuse.

4. Information Sharing

Client information may be shared with approved financial institutions and partners solely for the purpose of processing finance applications. We do not sell or distribute personal information to third parties.

5. Client Responsibility

Clients are responsible for ensuring that the information provided is accurate and up to date. Providing false information may affect approval outcomes.

6. Updates to This Policy

Auto Finance Group reserves the right to update this policy at any time. Changes will be reflected on this page.
